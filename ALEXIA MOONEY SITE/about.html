Alexia Mooney is a fiber artist
based out of Atlanta, Georgia. She is working on

with a concentration in Textiles with a minor in

work tends to be very detail orientated, but she also
focuses on new techniques and mixed media. Alexia
started off focusing on music and technical theatre but
switched to fine art while attending an accredited university.
Alexia finds inspiration in everyday life, from human interaction
to the color of the sky. She also draws a lot of inspiration from
different historical events. Most of her current work is
focusing on the African American experience. While young, Alexia
enjoyed doing art, crafts, and sewing- which she learned
from her mother and grandmother. Alexia makes art as a creative
outlet, source of information, and to get people talking about
controversial topics. Alexia posts most of her current and old
work on her Instagram @_lexcr8_ or her YouTube at Lexia Moon.
